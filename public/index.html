Local Anonymization Software for Partnered Hospitals

A lightweight, personalized desktop application designed to run locally on your hospital machines. Irreversibly strip PHI from ultrasound dicoms and lab reports before data ever leaves your network. Available on all Operating Systems.

  • Zero Cloud Footprint: All redaction occurs strictly offline on your own clinical hardware.
  • Low Requirements: Easily installs and runs on standard systems without heavy overhead.
  • Personalized Software: Pre-configured specifically for your hospital's workflow and IDs.
  • Personalized Development: Contact us for customized software development and workflow integrations.

Expected input structure

One patient folder, predictable subfolders

This structure helps the Anonymization tool to classify files consistently and produce an Anonymized package.

Patient_Folder/
├─ Left_Kidney_Images/ DICOM, JPEG, PNG
├─ Right_Kidney_Images/ DICOM, JPEG, PNG
├─ Ultrasound_Reports/ PDF or document files
└─ Lab_Reports/ PDF or document files
Irreversible
